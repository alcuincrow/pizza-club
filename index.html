Pizza & Book Club

This club is for people who love reading and eating pizza.
Members meet up bi-weekly at a local restaurant each week to discuss that week's book
and to share their love of pizza.

"The highlight of my month is enjoying wonderful pizza at a restaurant and discussing the latest book together with the group."
